Cars Daredevil Garage / Video Game

One of Disney's first mobile games that offered cross-play between an app and toy, Cars Daredevil Garage allowed players to grow their garage by scanning over 160 actual physical Mattel diecast cars into the app. The app also featured all the tracks, twists, and stunts that you would expect in a Cars game.
